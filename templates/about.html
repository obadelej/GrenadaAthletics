About Grenada Athletics Association

Promoting excellence in athletics across Grenada

Our Mission

The Grenada Athletics Association is dedicated to developing, promoting, and advancing track and field athletics throughout Grenada. We strive to provide opportunities for athletes of all levels to excel, compete, and achieve their full potential.

We are committed to fostering a culture of excellence, integrity, and sportsmanship while building a strong foundation for the future of athletics in our nation.

Our Vision

To be the premier athletics organization in the Caribbean, recognized for producing world-class athletes and hosting world-class events.

We envision a future where Grenada is synonymous with athletic excellence, where our athletes consistently compete at the highest international levels, and where our organization serves as a model for athletics development throughout the region.

Our Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of athletics, from training to competition to administration.

Integrity

We conduct our operations with honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior, ensuring fair play and clean competition.

Inclusivity

We welcome athletes of all ages, abilities, and backgrounds, fostering a diverse and inclusive athletics community.

Development

We invest in the growth and development of athletes, coaches, and officials to build a sustainable athletics program.

Passion

We are driven by a deep passion for athletics and a commitment to inspiring the next generation of athletes.

Achievement

We celebrate success at all levels and support our athletes in reaching their personal and competitive goals.

What We Do

Athlete Development

We provide comprehensive training programs, coaching support, and competitive opportunities for athletes at all stages of their development.

Event Organization

We organize and manage a wide range of athletic events, from local competitions to international meets, ensuring high-quality experiences for participants and spectators.

Coach Education

We offer training and certification programs for coaches, ensuring they have the knowledge and skills to effectively guide and develop athletes.

International Representation

We represent Grenada in international competitions and work with World Athletics to ensure our athletes have opportunities to compete on the global stage.

Anti-Doping Programs

We maintain strict anti-doping policies and education programs to ensure clean competition and protect the integrity of athletics.

Performance Tracking

We maintain comprehensive records of athlete performances, competition results, and rankings to track progress and celebrate achievements.

Our Legacy

The Grenada Athletics Association has a rich history of promoting excellence in track and field athletics. Over the years, we have produced numerous national champions, regional medalists, and international competitors who have proudly represented Grenada on the world stage.

Our organization continues to build on this legacy by providing modern facilities, comprehensive training programs, and opportunities for athletes to develop their skills and compete at the highest levels. We are proud to be part of the World Athletics family and remain committed to upholding the highest standards of the sport.

Whether you're a young athlete just starting out, a competitive athlete seeking to improve your performance, or a coach looking to develop your skills, the Grenada Athletics Association is here to support your journey.
